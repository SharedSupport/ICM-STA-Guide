Training types:

A
In-House Training - trainings specific to the individual you support
e.g. Who Is meetings, circle meetings, individual-specific training
B
General Training - organization-wide trainings done with Shared Support
e.g. Annual training, First Aid/CPR. Includes a dropdown of all Shared Support trainings - select the one that best fits. It can be corrected later if needed.

6
When training ends: select your name → Clock Out

Editing a Punch

🌐
Browser Required
  • Punch edits must be done in a web browser - this can be done on your phone's browser, an iPad, or a CLA computer
  • The mobile app itself does not support editing punches
1
In a browser, open iCM → select your name → My Timesheets
2
Find your name → click anywhere in your timesheet row
3
Scroll down to see all punches for the last two weeks
Status shows as Open or Submitted. Only Open punches can be edited.
4
Under Action, click the dropdown → select Edit
5
Adjust the time or other details as needed
6
Click SAVE
💾

Always SAVE - Never Submit

  • Click SAVE when finished entering or editing a punch
  • Do NOT click Submit - your Program Specialist (PS) will submit on your behalf after review
  • Once submitted, a punch is locked and cannot be edited
  • If you accidentally click Submit and need to make changes to the details, notify your PS immediately

Checking Your Hours & PTO Balance

1

2

3
Click the + button next to Time Off Balances to see PTO and any other time off balances
For example, if you have jury duty or bereavement hours available, you'll see those listed along with hours available for entry.

Requesting PTO / Time Off

🌐
Browser Required
  • PTO requests must be submitted in a web browser - this can be done on your phone's browser, an iPad, or a CLA computer
  • The mobile app itself does not support submitting PTO requests
ℹ️
How PTO Requests Work
  • Replaces paper PTO requests - submitting in iCM automatically notifies your House Supervisor and Program Specialist
  • The system automatically creates the punch for you once approved - you do not need to enter a separate punch
  • Your request will show Open status until the day of the PTO - this is normal, do not panic
  • Your PS will approve by adding a comment. The status won't change to Approved in the system until the pay period processes.
  • You can also delete your own PTO request if your plans change (e.g. you pick up a shift and no longer need it)
  • If your supervisor denies the request, they will cancel/delete it and your hours will be restored
  • For time off requested far in the future, your balance can show as negative. This means you will have the time off available by then, but don't have it yet as of today.
  • Submitting immediately reduces your displayed balance - this is expected. Check UKG for your accurate balance for now.
  • Jury duty or bereavement hours: Do not request through iCM - go through Maxine (HR)
1
Select your name → My Time Off Requests
2
Select My Request Time Off → choose PTO
3
Select Hours → enter the number of hours needed
e.g., enter 2 hours if you need to leave early
4
Add a comment with the name of the individual you support and the reason
e.g. "John Smith - leaving 2 hours early for appointment"
5
Click Save and Submit
For time-off requests, Save and Submit is correct - unlike punch entries.
1
Select your name → My Time Off Requests
2
Select My Request Time Off → choose PTO
3
Select Hours → pick the first day → enter 8 hours
4
Click the + button to add the next day → enter 8 hours → repeat for each day
You're able to go into a negative balance for time off requested in the future - this just means you don't have the hours available yet as of today.
5
Add a comment with the individual's name and dates (e.g. "John Smith - vacation 6/22 to 6/28")
6
Click Save and Submit

Transportation Log

ℹ️
Log in Real Time
  • Always enter transportation logs in real time
  • Only add entries after the fact if you had no service or iCM was down
  • For personal vehicles, log each individual trip separately
1
Navigate to Transportation Log → click Add Entry
2
Enter Start Date and Start Time for this trip
3
Enter End Date and End Time for this trip
4
Enter Starting Odometer and Ending Odometer for this trip
Accurate odometer readings are required for mileage reimbursement.
5
Enter Starting Location and Ending Location
6
Select the Individual being transported or supported
7
Enter the Purpose of the trip (locations visited)
8
Click Save - then repeat from Step 1 for your next trip
Each trip must be logged separately so mileage reimbursement is calculated accurately.
1
Navigate to Fleet Management → Vehicles
2
Select the Program → click Search → find the vehicle for your individual
3
Select the vehicle → find Distance Log → click Add
4
Enter the Starting Odometer at the beginning of your day and the Ending Odometer at the end of your day
Company vehicle mileage is logged once per day as a total - not per individual trip.
5
Enter Start Date/Time and End Date/Time for the day
6
Select the Individual → check "I completed my pre-trip vehicle inspection"
This checkbox is required.
7
Enter Purpose of the trip (locations visited throughout the day) → click Save
1
Open the iCM app → tap Transportation Log (blue tab) → select Agency
2
Tap the QR code button (top right) → scan the QR code on the driver's side of the vehicle
3
Tap Add Entry (top right) at the start of your day
4
Enter the Starting Odometer - take a photo or type manually
Manual entry is available if the photo doesn't capture correctly.
5
Confirm date and time → select the Individual → check pre-trip inspection box → tap Save → tap Done
6
At the end of your day, tap End Trip (top right) → enter the End Odometer reading
Company vehicle mileage is logged once per day as a total - not per individual trip.
7
Enter Purpose of the trip (all locations visited during the day) → tap Save
1
Tap Transportation Log (blue tab) → select Personal Vehicle → tap Add Entry
2
Enter date, time, and Starting Odometer for this trip (photo or manual)
Accurate odometer readings are required for mileage reimbursement.
3
Enter Starting Location → tap Save → tap Done
4
When the trip ends: tap End Trip → enter End Date, End Time, End Odometer, Ending Location, and Purpose of trip → tap Save
5
Repeat from Step 1 for your next trip
Each trip must be logged separately so mileage reimbursement is calculated accurately.
